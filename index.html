About Nairobi United FC

Our Story

Founded in 2015 and rebranded in 2022, Nairobi United FC has rapidly become one of Kenya's most promising football clubs. Under the visionary leadership of the Sakaja Foundation since 2024, we are committed to excellence both on and off the pitch.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to promote youth development, football excellence, and community engagement. We envision becoming a leading football institution in Africa, nurturing local talent and competing at the highest levels.
